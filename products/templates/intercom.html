Intercom

The system consists of wire color hands free video phone/ Intercom for Gate station. The system is not only convenient to use but also designed to provide an additional measure of security that brings advanced innovative technology in your home or business. For commercial operations where access control is a concern this system, safe guards your entrance while remaining user friendly to guests. CHF series hands free color intercom system is the first in the industry to incorporate day and night camera to accommodate lighting environment. This system operates in color during day light and black & white in low lighting conditions. The built- in infrared provides supplemental lighting to enhance night viewing.

Features Clear image and high resolution 7inch color digital LCD, Compact and slim design, Easy and simple to install, Connection up to 2door cameras and 2 monitor with sub audio unit Automatic connection between monitor and camera Call and communication with visitor and door open function
